Privacy Policy for African Sun Concierge Services Company

African Sun Concierge Services Company (hereafter referred to as "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose personal information by the South African Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Collection of Personal Information

We only collect personal information that is necessary for our business purposes and to provide our concierge services to you. The personal information we may collect includes, but is not limited to:

•   Your name, address, and contact details (e.g., phone number, email address)

•   Identification information (e.g., passport number, ID number)

•   Financial information (e.g., credit card details)

•   Travel-related information (e.g., flight details, hotel reservations)

We may collect personal information directly from you or third parties (e.g., travel agents, and airlines).

2. Use of Personal Information

We use personal information to provide our concierge services to you, including but not limited to:

•   Making travel arrangements (e.g., booking flights, making hotel reservations)

•   Providing local recommendations and reservations (e.g., restaurant recommendations, booking tours)

•   Providing assistance with general errands and tasks (e.g., grocery shopping, laundry)

We may also use personal information to:

•   Communicate with you regarding our services

•   Conduct market research and analysis to improve our services

•   Comply with legal obligations

3. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not disclose personal information to third parties unless required by law or necessary to provide our concierge services to you. We may disclose personal information to third parties such as:

•   Travel suppliers (e.g., airlines, hotels) to make travel arrangements

•   Local service providers (e.g., restaurants, tour operators) to make reservations

•   Our service providers (e.g., IT providers, and marketing agencies) help us provide our services.

•   Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

4. Protection of Personal Information

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We have implemented policies and procedures to ensure that personal information is kept secure and confidential.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and update the personal information that we hold. You also have the right to request that we delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes to this privacy policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at info@africansun.co.
